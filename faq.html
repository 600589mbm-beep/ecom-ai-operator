FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need a paid Claude account?

You need access to Claude — either Claude Code (CLI) or a Claude.ai plan that supports Projects. The skills run on your own account, so you're never paying us a usage fee.

How do I install the skills?

Claude Code: copy each skill folder into ~/.claude/skills/ and restart. Claude.ai: paste a skill's instructions into a Project, or upload the folder. The included guide walks you through it in two minutes.

Will it work for my niche?

Yes. Each skill asks for your product facts and brand voice, so it adapts to any physical-product store — supplements aside, where ad/health claims are restricted (the skills flag those for you).

Is this really one-time?

Starter and Pro are one-time purchases with 12 months of updates. The Operator Club is the only recurring option, and it's optional — for people who want a new skill every month.

Can I use it for multiple stores or clients?

The standard license covers a single store. If you're an agency running client stores, reply to your purchase email about an agency license.

What's your refund policy?

30 days, no questions asked. Email and we refund.

Are you affiliated with Anthropic?

No. We build skills for Claude; we're an independent product. "Claude" is a trademark of Anthropic, PBC.
